About Us

Who are we?

The NGO PetConnect is an animal welfare non-profit organization which works for the rescue of stray, abandoned as well as injured animals such as dogs,cats, etc. We, at the PetConnect organization, truly believe that animals are the real victims at times. “NO ANIMAL LEFT BEHIND” is our motto.Keeping the ever rising cases of accidents,cruelty, starvation,illness and so on we strive to rescue such animals in danger.PetConnect organization also supports feeding stray dogs,cats in need and also appreciates all the local street animal feeders.
