ADOPTION

Why adopt a pet?

"A Hand For a Paw" is what we believe in. If you want to adopt a pet and contribute to our work to continue rescuing animals. Please schedule a visit to our organization. Most of the time, we rescue animals that have been abandoned or mistreated.We in here facilitate foster care,active and passive adoption.People with concern for animals are always welcome to adopt these homeless pets and can also give some of their time and love to these pets. Feel free to contact us for more details.

Do check out all adoption services we provide.
